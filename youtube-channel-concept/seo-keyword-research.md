## Thumbnail SEO Notes

YouTube thumbnails directly affect CTR (click-through rate) and thus SEO rank.

### Thumbnail best practices for Money Mind:
1. **Always show a face** (animated character) — faces increase CTR by 38%
2. **Use contrasting colors** — navy + gold stands out in the algorithm's recommended grid
3. **3–5 words max** on the thumbnail — legible on mobile (40% of views)
4. **Imply a before/after** — stressed character OR confident character, not neutral
5. **A/B test** with YouTube Studio after 48 hours — swap if CTR below 4%

### CTR targets by view phase:
- 0–1,000 views: CTR above 6% = strong thumbnail
- 1,000–10,000 views: CTR above 4% = keep it
- 10,000+ views: CTR above 3% = YouTube is showing it broadly

### What works for them:
- High-contrast thumbnails (red/yellow/black)
- 2D animated characters with expressive faces
- Titles with dollar amounts ("$500,000 by 30")
- Comparison format consistently performs
- 8–15 minute sweet spot for main content

### Differentiation for Money Mind:
- **Navy + gold branding** (premium, not alarming)
- **Word-for-word scripts** (more actionable than competitors)
- **Real math, every video** (calculators, compound growth charts)
- **Myth-Bust format** (unique to our Sunday slot)
- **Automation focus** (how to make finances automatic, not willpower-based)
