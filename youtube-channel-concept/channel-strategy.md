# Money Mind – Complete YouTube Growth Strategy

## Posting Schedule

### Upload Time: Every Tuesday at 10:00 AM EST
```
Why Tuesday at 10 AM?
- Tuesday is the highest-traffic weekday on YouTube
- 10 AM catches people during morning commutes and lunch breaks
- Algorithm rewards early engagement — the first 2 hours are critical
- Avoids competition with Monday rushes and weekend drops
```

### Weekly Content Calendar
```
Monday      → Script finalized + thumbnail ready
Tuesday     → Upload at 10:00 AM sharp
Wednesday   → Post Instagram Reel (60-sec clip from the video)
Thursday    → Post TikTok version (60-sec hook only)
Friday      → Reply to all comments from Tuesday's video
Saturday    → Research + outline next video
Sunday      → Film voiceover + start animation
```

## The 10 AM Upload Checklist

Before hitting "Publish" every Tuesday at 10:00 AM, confirm:

### Video
- [ ] Video length: 10–20 minutes (optimal for AdSense)
- [ ] No dead air longer than 3 seconds
- [ ] Hook lands in first 30 seconds
- [ ] CTA appears at 60% mark and again at the end
- [ ] End screen set up (subscribe button + next video)
- [ ] Cards added at key moments

### Metadata
- [ ] Title: under 60 characters, curiosity-driven
- [ ] Description: 300+ words, keyword-rich, timestamps included
- [ ] Tags: 10–15 relevant tags (mix of broad + specific)
- [ ] Category set to: Education
- [ ] Language set to: English

### Thumbnail
- [ ] 1280×720 pixels, under 2MB
- [ ] Max 6 words on thumbnail
- [ ] High contrast text vs background
- [ ] Character face clearly visible
- [ ] Tested at small size (mobile preview)

### Extras
- [ ] Pinned comment written and ready to post immediately after upload
- [ ] Community post scheduled for 12:00 PM same day
- [ ] Short clip exported for TikTok/Reels

## The Perfect Video Formula

### Hook Styles That Work (First 30 Seconds)
```
TYPE 1 – Shocking Stat:
"The average American spends $700,000 on cars in their lifetime.
That's more than most people's retirement savings."

TYPE 2 – Controversial Statement:
"Your financial advisor might actually be making you poorer.
Here's the proof."

TYPE 3 – Direct Challenge:
"If you have money sitting in a savings account right now,
you're losing money. And I can prove it in 60 seconds."

TYPE 4 – Story Hook:
"Two years ago I made a single financial decision that cost me $40,000.
Today I'm going to show you exactly what it was — so you don't do the same."
```

## Analytics & KPIs to Track Weekly

### Every Wednesday Morning – Review These Numbers:

| Metric | What It Tells You | Target |
|--------|------------------|--------|
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | Is your thumbnail/title working? | 6–10% |
| Average View Duration | Is your content holding attention? | 50%+ |
| Impressions | Is YouTube pushing your video? | Growing |
| Subscribers gained | Net new from this video | Positive |
| Comments | Engagement quality | 1 per 100 views |
| Shares | Virality potential | Track trend |

### Monthly Review Questions:
1. Which video got the most views? Why?
2. Which video had the best CTR? What was different about the thumbnail?
3. Which video had the best Average View Duration? Why did people watch more?
4. What topics did viewers ask about in comments?
5. Are we on track for subscriber goal?

## The Golden Rules

```
✅ Upload EVERY Tuesday at 10:00 AM — no exceptions
✅ Thumbnail and title are 80% of your success — spend 20% of time on them
✅ First 30 seconds = the hook — spend 30% of script time on it
✅ Respond to every comment in the first 48 hours
✅ Never end a video without a CTA (like, subscribe, watch next)
✅ Optimize old videos every month (update title, thumbnail, description)
✅ Study your analytics every Wednesday — data over assumptions
✅ One niche only — don't mix topics (stay personal finance)
✅ Post consistently — 1 video/week beats 3 videos/week for 2 weeks then nothing
✅ Copy what works — study top-performing finance channels every month
```
