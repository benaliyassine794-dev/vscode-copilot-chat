## WHERE TO ACTUALLY INVEST IT (3:30 – 5:30)

**[Scene: Character at a simple, clean investing dashboard]**

VOICEOVER:
"So how does Sam actually do this? Here are the exact steps.

**Step 1: Open a tax-advantaged account.**
In the US, that's a Roth IRA or 401k. In the UK, that's an ISA. In most countries, there's an equivalent.

These accounts let your investments grow tax-free or tax-deferred. That's legally free money.

**Step 2: Buy a total market or S&P 500 index fund.**
My favorites: VTI, VOO, or FZROX. Low fees. Instant diversification across 500+ companies.

You don't need to pick stocks. You don't need to watch charts. You just buy the whole market.

**Step 3: Automate it.**
Set up an automatic transfer on the day you get paid. You never see the money, you never miss it.

This is called 'paying yourself first.' It's the single most effective personal finance habit.

**Step 4: Never touch it.**
This is the hard part. When markets crash — and they will — do not sell.

The people who panic-sell during crashes are the ones who never retire wealthy."
