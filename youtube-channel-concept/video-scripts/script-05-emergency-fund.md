## THE 30-DAY PLAN (1:30 – 4:30)
**[Scene: Calendar with daily targets filling up]**

VOICEOVER:
"$1,000 in 30 days = **$33.33 per day**.

That sounds impossible. But watch this:

**Week 1 — Find the money:**
- Cancel unused subscriptions: $50–$150/month
- Sell 5 things you don't use (eBay, Facebook Marketplace): $100–$300
- Skip restaurants for 1 week: $80–$150
- Week 1 total: **$230–$600**

**Week 2 — Reduce spending:**
- Cook all meals at home: save $200–$400
- No entertainment purchases: save $50–$100
- Walk or bike instead of Uber: save $30–$80
- Week 2 total: **$280–$580**

**Week 3 — Add income:**
- Offer one skill on Fiverr or TaskRabbit: $100–$500
- Work one extra shift or overtime: $100–$300
- Return unused purchases: $50–$200
- Week 3 total: **$250–$1,000**

**Week 4 — Finish strong:**
- Any gap remaining? Do a 24-hour no-spend challenge
- Transfer whatever you have to a separate savings account
- Done."

## THE GOLDEN RULE (4:30 – 5:30)
**[Scene: Padlock clicks shut on savings jar]**

VOICEOVER:
"One rule: this money is **not for wants**. Only for true emergencies.

Not a sale. Not a trip. Not a gift.

A real emergency: job loss, medical bill, essential car repair, broken appliance that stops your life.

Put it in a separate account — not your checking account. Make it slightly inconvenient to access. That friction is the point."
