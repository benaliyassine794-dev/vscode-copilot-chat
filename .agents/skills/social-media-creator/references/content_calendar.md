# Content Calendar & Strategy

## Content Pillars Framework
Every channel needs 3–5 recurring pillars (creates expectation, enables batching).

**Example — Digital Marketing creator:**
1. **Education** — Tutorials, how-tos, explainers (40%)
2. **Inspiration** — Success stories, transformations (20%)
3. **Entertainment** — BTS, opinions, fails (20%)
4. **Promotion** — Products, services, collabs (10%)
5. **Community** — Q&A, challenges, polls (10%)

## 30-Day Launch Calendar

**Week 1 — Establish Authority**
- Day 1: Pillar (Education) — your best tutorial
- Day 3: Pillar (Inspiration) — transformation story
- Day 5: Trending topic in your niche
- Day 7: Community — poll or Q&A

**Week 2 — Build Discovery**
- Day 8: SEO-optimized search content
- Day 10: Collab or duet/stitch
- Day 12: Trending sound/format adapted to niche
- Day 14: Authentic behind-the-scenes

**Week 3 — Deepen Engagement**
- Day 15: Deep-dive (high save/bookmark value)
- Day 17: Opinion / controversy piece
- Day 19: Serialized content ("Part 2 of...")
- Day 21: UGC prompt or community challenge

**Week 4 — Convert & Retain**
- Day 22: Value-packed cheat sheet or resource
- Day 24: Live stream or Q&A
- Day 26: Best-of recap or compilation
- Day 28: Tease upcoming content
- Day 30: Review analytics, plan next month

## Content Repurposing Matrix
| Source            | Repurpose Into                     | Platform           |
|-------------------|------------------------------------|--------------------||
| YouTube video     | 3–5 short clips (best moments)    | TikTok/Reels/Shorts|
| Video script      | Twitter/X thread                   | X/Twitter          |
| Key points        | Carousel (1 point per slide)       | Instagram/LinkedIn |
| Transcript        | Blog post or LinkedIn article      | Website/LinkedIn   |
| Data/stats        | Infographic pin                    | Pinterest          |
| BTS footage       | Story sequence                     | Instagram/Snapchat |
| Quote             | Quote graphic                      | Instagram/Facebook |
| Summary           | Email newsletter section           | Email              |

## Trending Content Sources
**Daily (5 min):** TikTok Creative Center → trending sounds + hashtags | YouTube Trending tab
**Weekly (15 min):** Google Trends keyword variants | Exploding Topics | Reddit r/[yourniche]
**Monthly (30 min):** SparkToro audience research | BuzzSumo top shared content

## Posting Frequency Guide
| Platform  | Minimum   | Recommended | Aggressive   |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| TikTok    | 3x/week   | 1x/day      | 2–3x/day    |
| Instagram | 3x/week   | 5x/week     | 1x/day      |
| YouTube   | 1x/week   | 2x/week     | 3x/week     |
| LinkedIn  | 2x/week   | 5x/week     | 1x/day      |
| X/Twitter | 3x/week   | 2x/day      | 5x/day      |
| Pinterest | 5 pins/day| 15 pins/day | 25 pins/day |
| Facebook  | 3x/week   | 5x/week     | 1x/day      |

**Rule:** Consistency > frequency. 3x/week reliably beats 7x/week for 2 weeks then stopping.

## Content Batching System
**Day 1 — Create:** film all video content (group by location/outfit), record voiceovers, capture B-roll
**Day 2 — Produce:** edit all videos, write all captions, design all thumbnails/covers, schedule everything
**Tools:** CapCut (mobile editing) | DaVinci Resolve (desktop) | Canva (graphics) | Buffer or Later (scheduling)
