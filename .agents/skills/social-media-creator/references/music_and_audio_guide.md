# Music & Audio Guide for Social Media

## Platform Audio Features

### TikTok
- **Sounds library:** 500K+ licensed tracks + user-uploaded sounds
- **Trending sounds:** TikTok Creative Center → Trending → Sounds (filter by region)
- **Duet with sound:** use original audio to piggyback on viral sounds
- **Voiceover tool:** built-in TTS in 10+ languages
- **Strategy:** trending sound = built-in discovery boost; use in first 3s

### Instagram Reels
- **Music library:** same catalog as TikTok for personal accounts; Business accounts have limited library
- **Original audio:** name your audio → becomes searchable if viral
- **Add music to Stories:** sticker → Music → search or trending
- **Collab audio:** use the same audio as an influencer to ride their wave
- **Tip:** Business accounts → switch to Creator for full music access

### YouTube
- **Audio Library:** free music and SFX in YouTube Studio (royalty-free, safe for monetization)
- **Shorts audio:** same trending sounds mechanic as TikTok
- **Copyright:** Content ID system — use licensed music only or claim will mute/monetize your video
- **BGM tips:** instrumental, 60–80 BPM for tutorials; energetic for lifestyle vlogs

### Facebook
- **Sound Collection:** royalty-free tracks in Creator Studio
- **Reels audio:** shared library with Instagram
- **Live music:** licensed through Facebook's agreements (limited)

### Snapchat
- **Spotlight audio:** trending sounds boost discovery
- **Stories:** add music via Snap's licensed library

### LinkedIn
- No music library; focus on clear voice-over audio quality

### Pinterest
- Idea Pins support background music from licensed library

## Music Licensing Explained

### Safe ✅
- Music from the platform's own library (YouTube Audio Library, TikTok Sounds)
- Royalty-free from Epidemic Sound / Artlist with active subscription
- Creative Commons (CC0) — no attribution required
- Music you composed/own

### Risky ⚠️
- Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY) — must credit
- Royalty-free without reading the exact license (some restrict commercial use)

### Avoid ❌
- Commercial music without a license (Spotify tracks, pop songs)
- Music from "royalty-free" sites that actually require one-time fees you haven't paid
- Cover songs without a cover license

## Audio Branding

**Sonic identity** = the sound your brand is always associated with.

1. **Intro jingle:** 2–5s recognizable sound at video start (earworm)
2. **Consistent BGM:** same playlist/artist style across all videos
3. **Voice character:** consistent tone, pace, and energy in voice-overs
4. **SFX library:** branded sound effects for transitions, text pop-ins
5. **Outro music:** same track every video → builds Pavlovian recognition

**Tools for custom audio branding:**
- Suno AI / Udio — generate original music from text prompts
- Adobe Audition — professional audio editing
- Audacity — free audio editing
- Descript — AI voice + audio editor
- ElevenLabs — AI voice cloning for consistent voice-overs

## Trending Sound Strategy

1. **Find the trend:** TikTok Creative Center → Trending Sounds (top 100, by region)
2. **Check age:** sounds 1–3 days old = early adopter advantage; 7+ days = declining
3. **Adapt to your niche:** use the trending sound but apply YOUR topic/content
4. **Don't force it:** irrelevant audio hurts watch time → skip if no natural fit
5. **Original audio play:** create a unique sound that others duet/stitch with

## Audio Quality Checklist
- [ ] No background hum/noise (use noise reduction in editing)
- [ ] Voice-over at -12 to -6 dB, BGM at -20 to -30 dB (voice always dominant)
- [ ] Consistent volume across clips (no sudden loud/quiet shifts)
- [ ] Captions added (85% of mobile viewers watch without sound)
- [ ] Intro audio hooks within 1s
