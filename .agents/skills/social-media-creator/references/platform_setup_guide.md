# Platform Setup Guide

## YouTube
- **Name:** 50 chars max, include primary keyword naturally
- **Handle:** @username 3–30 chars, consistent across platforms
- **Description:** 1000 chars, primary keyword in first 100 chars
- **Channel art:** 2560×1440px (safe zone 1546×423px center)
- **Profile photo:** 800×800px, recognizable at 98×98px
- **Trailer:** 45–90s, hook in first 5s
- **Tags:** 15 tags in Settings → Channel → Basic Info
- **Monetization:** 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours OR 10M Shorts views (90 days)

## TikTok
- **Username:** consistent with other platforms
- **Bio:** 80 chars max, keyword + emoji + CTA + link
- **Profile photo:** 200×200px min, face or clear logo
- **Account type:** Creator Account for analytics + monetization
- **Auto-captions:** ON (improves watch time and indexing)
- **Creator Rewards:** 10K followers + 100K views/30 days + 18+

## Instagram
- **Username:** keyword-searchable if possible
- **Name field:** includes keyword — THIS IS SEARCHABLE (not just handle)
- **Bio:** 150 chars, value line 1, CTA line 2, link line 3
- **Profile photo:** upload 320×320px (displays 110×110px)
- **Account type:** Creator (analytics + music) or Business (ads + shopping)
- **Content hierarchy:** Reels (reach) > Carousels (saves) > Stories (retention)

## X / Twitter
- **Bio:** 160 chars, niche keyword + social proof + personality
- **Header:** 1500×500px
- **Profile photo:** 400×400px
- **Pin:** best-performing tweet always pinned
- **X Premium:** verification badge + longer posts + Analytics+

## Facebook
- **Category:** choose most specific relevant category
- **Username:** claim @handle immediately
- **Short description:** 255 chars with keywords
- **Cover:** 851×315px | Profile: 170×170px
- **CTA button:** set to Follow / Learn More / Shop Now
- **Meta Business Suite:** manage Instagram + Facebook together

## LinkedIn
- **Headline:** 220 chars, keyword-rich value statement (NOT just job title)
- **About:** 2600 chars, hook in first 300 chars (visible before "see more")
- **Creator Mode:** ON — enables Follow button + newsletter + analytics
- **Featured section:** pin top posts, articles, external links
- **Company page specialties:** 20 keywords (searchable)

## Snapchat
- **Display name:** 75 chars | Bio: 150 chars
- **Story settings:** Public
- **Spotlight:** 9:16, 60s max, no watermarks from other platforms
- **Snapcode:** download and share for cross-platform promotion

## Pinterest
- **Business account:** required for analytics and ads
- **Claimed website:** verify domain for SEO boost
- **Board names:** descriptive + keyword (rank in Google)
- **Pin size:** 1000×1500px (2:3 ratio)
- **Idea Pins:** up to 20 pages, highest engagement format
- **Description:** 500 chars, keyword in first 100 chars
