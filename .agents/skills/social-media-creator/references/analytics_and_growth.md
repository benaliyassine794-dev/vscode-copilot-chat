# Analytics & Growth Guide

## Key Metrics by Platform

### YouTube
| Metric                  | Benchmark        | Target |
|-------------------------|-----------------|--------|
| Click-through rate (CTR)| 2–10%           | >5%    |
| Average view duration   | 30–50% of length| >40%   |
| Subscriber conversion   | 0.5–2% of viewers| >1%   |

**CTR diagnosis:** <2% → fix thumbnail/title | 2–5% → optimize thumbnail | >8% → focus on watch time

### TikTok
| Metric             | Benchmark | Target |
|--------------------|----------|--------|
| For You Page rate  | 40–60%   | >70%   |
| Average watch %    | 50–80%   | >65%   |
| Follower conversion| 1–5%     | >3%    |
| Share rate         | 1–3%     | >2%    |

**Virality threshold:** 20%+ views from non-followers → TikTok pushes the video

### Instagram
| Metric               | Benchmark   | Target  |
|----------------------|------------|----------|
| Reach rate (Reels)   | 10–30% of followers | >25% |
| Engagement rate      | 1–5%       | >3%      |
| Save rate            | 0.5–3%     | >1.5%    |
| Story completion     | 60–80%     | >70%     |

### LinkedIn
| Metric            | Benchmark        |
|-------------------|------------------|
| Impressions/post  | 500–5,000        |
| Engagement rate   | 2–5%             |
| Follower growth   | 1–3%/month       |

## Content Audit (Monthly)
1. Export analytics → sort by views/reach
2. Identify top 20% (what worked) and bottom 20% (what failed)
3. Extract patterns: topics, formats, lengths, posting times
4. Double down on top patterns, cut bottom patterns

## Growth Tactics by Stage

### 0 → 1,000 Followers
- Post frequency: 1x/day TikTok, 5x/week YouTube/Instagram
- Reply to every comment in the first hour (algorithm signal)
- Same-size collabs outperform big-creator collabs at this stage
- Trend-ride: make your niche version of trending sounds/formats

### 1,000 → 10,000 Followers
- Find your "pillar content" (1 format that consistently hits)
- SEO-optimized content for search discovery
- Shorts/Reels as top-of-funnel to long-form
- Cross-post with platform-specific edits (not raw reposts)

### 10,000 → 100,000 Followers
- Batch-produce 4 weeks ahead; hire or outsource editing
- Newsletter + community (convert audience to owned channels)
- Boost best organic posts ($20–$50 to test paid reach)
- Collab with larger creators (value exchange)

## A/B Testing Framework
Test one variable at a time, 2-week cycles:
1. **Thumbnail:** same video, different cover (YouTube allows this)
2. **Title:** edit after 48h if CTR < 3%
3. **Hook:** film 2 openings, test retention past 30s
4. **Posting time:** same content type, different day/hour
5. **Format:** talking head vs screen record vs B-roll heavy

## Competitor Analysis Checklist
- [ ] List top 10 creators in your niche
- [ ] Note posting frequency and dominant formats
- [ ] Identify highest-performing content (filter by Views, last 30 days)
- [ ] Extract title patterns and thumbnail styles
- [ ] Find gaps: topics they haven't covered or covered poorly
- [ ] Study the PATTERN, not the content — don't copy directly
